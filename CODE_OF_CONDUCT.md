# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and constructive environment for all contributors and users.

## Standards

- Be respectful and considerate
- Focus on technical merit, not personal opinions
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Keep discussions practical and actionable

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to remove inappropriate comments, issues, or contributions.

This project is small and focused. If something needs attention, open an issue.
